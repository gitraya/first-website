Grand Theft Auto: San Andreas

Written by Raya april 27, 2020

GTA San Andreas a is also one of my favorite games, which I played when I was in elementary school also accompanied my days, I remember that every time I came home from school I went to a PS2 rental near home, and paid IDR 2,000 per hour just to be able to play GTA SA, and continue the missions that I have not yet completed, sometimes I also play with friends in multiplayer mode, just so much fun playing gta at that time.


Synopsis

"Five years ago Carl Johnson escaped from the pressures of life in Los Santos, San Andreas... a city tearing itself apart with gang trouble, drugs and corruption. Where filmstars and millionaires do their best to avoid the dealers and gangbangers.

Now, it's the early 90s. Carl's got to go home. His mother has been murdered, his family has fallen apart and his childhood friends are all heading towards disaster.

On his return to the neighborhood, a couple of corrupt cops frame him for homicide. CJ is forced on a journey that takes him across the entire state of San Andreas, to save his family and to take control of the streets."
